Company Overview

Monas Petroleum Marketing Nigeria Limited is a dynamic and fast-growing player in Nigeria's downstream oil and gas sector. We specialize in the marketing and distribution of refined petroleum products including Premium Motor Spirit (PMS), Automotive Gas Oil (AGO), Dual Purpose Kerosene (DPK), and Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG). With a strong focus on efficiency, safety, and customer satisfaction, Monas Petroleum is committed to powering communities, industries, and economic growth across Nigeria through wisdom-driven energy solutions that deliver measurable value to our partners and stakeholders.
